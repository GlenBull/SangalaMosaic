Sangala Mosaic

Sangala Mosaic turns a photo into a mosaic that can be built by hand from LEGO tiles on a baseplate. It is part of the course Introduction to Design through Making. The word “sangala” means joy in the Lugisu language of the Mt. Elgon region in East Africa.

It shares its look with Sangala Studio but is a separate tool, kept apart so neither one grows unwieldy.
